About us

There are us, and there are you who have already studied somewhere to become programmers, testers, designers, project managers...

And we have to do something very important - something you can't do yourself - get your first job in IT.

So, we will help you, and each of you will then help three other beginners.

You will say: "It is difficult - is it not possible to plan everything in advance?".

Yes - we need to be more attentive to each other, protect each other. Because people don't always know what they really need. It's like an opportunity to fix something bigger than a bicycle, as the twelve-year-old boy in the famous novel said.

Tens of thousands of people annually complete their studies at universities, colleges, courses, laboratories, schools of IT companies or study IT specialties independently. But they cannot get their first job in the IT industry - as programmers, testers, web designers, project managers...
